About

UCI Quantitative Management

A new student organization at UC Irvine focused on algorithmic and quantitative trading.

UCI Quantitative Management was started to give UCI students a place to seriously study how financial markets work, not just in theory, but by building real strategies and running them. We operate like a small research team: structured, documented, and collaborative.
