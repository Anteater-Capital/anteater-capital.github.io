About

Anteater Capital

A new student organization at UC Irvine focused on algorithmic and quantitative trading.

Anteater Capital was started to give UCI students a place to seriously study how financial markets work — not just in theory, but by building real strategies and running them. We operate like a small research team: structured, documented, and collaborative.

The club is open to everyone regardless of major or background. Whether you're interested in the math, the engineering, the economics, or just curious about markets, there's a place for you here.

01 Tracks

📊

Research

Develop and backtest trading strategies. Write up your process and present results to the group for peer review.

💻

Engineering

Build the club's infrastructure — backtesting engines, data pipelines, paper trading integrations, and dashboards.

🏫

Education

Run weekly sessions and create learning resources for members who are newer to quantitative finance.
