01 What we do

    


    

      
Quantitative Research

      

        

          
📊

          
Statistical Signal Research

          
Find and test quantitative signals in market data. Members develop alpha from scratch — momentum, mean-reversion, cross-sectional factors — and validate them rigorously before anything goes live.

        

        

          
📈

          
Backtesting & Paper Trading

          
Every strategy runs through a structured backtest and then paper trades against the club's shared portfolio. Results are tracked and reviewed openly week over week.

        

      

    


    

      
Trading Systems Engineering

      

        

          
⚙️

          
Trading Systems in Rust

          
Build low-latency execution and data infrastructure in Rust. Order routing, position management, and real-time market data handling — engineered for performance and reliability.

        

        

          
🚀

          
Scalable Data Pipelines

          
Design and maintain the systems that power the club's research — ingestion, normalization, and storage of price and alternative data at scale. The plumbing every quant fund depends on.

        

      

    


    

      
Education

      

        

          
📚

          
Structured Curriculum

          
A progressive learning track covering probability, statistics, time-series analysis, and portfolio theory — with hands-on coding exercises tied directly to what members are building in the club.

        

        

          
💬

          
Research Seminars

          
Weekly sessions breaking down academic papers, industry strategies, and real market events. Members present their own work and get direct feedback from the group.

        

      

    

  


  


  
  

    
02 How it works

    

      

        
Phase 1

        
Learn & Build

        
Pick a strategy idea, research the signal, and implement it in Python. We provide the infrastructure — data feeds, a backtesting framework, and weekly sessions to help you get started.

        Hands-on from day one
        

      

      

        
Phase 2

        
Paper Trade Club Capital

        
Once your backtest holds up, your strategy goes live against the club's shared paper portfolio. Performance is tracked, compared, and reviewed openly by the group every week.

        Real execution, zero risk
        

      

      

        
Phase 3

        
Deploy on Your Own

        
Strategies that prove out over time can be deployed on your own brokerage account. The long-term goal is a pooled student fund — managed entirely by club members.

        Real capital, real returns
